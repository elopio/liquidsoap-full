h2. Some Savonet/Liquidsoap users

We know the following netradios to use Liquidsoap in various ways:

* "Art En Sort":http://artensort.com/ is an association to promote music in Normandy.
* "BAZE☆FM":http://baze.fm: an Hungarian Hip-Hop & R'n'B station, declaring 3 years of stable running.
* "Cool Guy Radio":http://coolguyradio.com/ a radio broadcasting improvised electronic music from Denver, Colorado.
* "DEADMEDIAFM":http://www.deadmediafm.org/ is a free form, open community streaming radio and podcast from Indonesia.
* "Deliradio":http://www.deliradio.it/ is a live web radio located in Rome, with many DJs and speakers from all Italy and many flavours of music.
* "Eargazm":http://eargazm.com is playing a mix of all flavors of rock, scads of jazz, a bit of fusion, a teaspoon of folk and a dash of comedy...
* "Eastanbul Media Üssü":http://www.eastanbul.com/, a Turkish radio streaming mostly electronic music.
* "Fréquence 3":doc-dev/frequence3.html, the first French webradio, broadcasting current hits, gold, mainly Pop/Rock
* "Geek radio":doc-dev/geekradio.html, the one for which Savonet was created
* "Infusio.net":http://infusio.net is a deep and dub radio, broadcasting free music from netlabels.
* "KUBE":http://www.kuberadio.com/, a student run radio at Keele University
* "La Noche del Hombre Lobo":http://lanochedelhombrelobo.org/, a Spanish Net-radio dedicated to the promotion of music under Creative Commons licenses.
* "Let's Go Zik":http://www.letsgozik.org, a French webradio dedicated to club & underground music (Trance, Techno, Hand's Up, HardStyle, Electro, House, ...) with a lot of live DJ set.
* "LiveATC":http://liveatc.net/ is streaming live air traffic communications from all over the world -- with single and synchronous/combined audio streams.
* "Loudcaster":http://loudcaster.com is for people who want to broadcast radio on-line, and people who want to listen and interact with the broadcasters.
* "NIA":http://www.nia.nc/, a French electronic radio from New-Calédonia
* "Overfitting disco":http://overfitted.ddcr.biz/ streams all sorts of disco
* "Oxyradio":http://www.oxyradio.net/ is a French associative netradio playing free/libre music, relying entirely on liquidsoap for all streaming-related tasks
* "Pi-Radio":http://piradio.de: a netradio and part-time FM broadcaster in Berlin, animated by an open community currently consisting of 30 independent groups
* "Podradio":http://www.podradio.fr/ A French netradio broadcasting live streams generated from various podcasts.
* "Pytagor":http://www.radiopytagor.com/, a French local FM radio station (Adult CHR)
* "Radio Groovalizacion":http://www.groovalizacion.com/ wants to give a voice to the new musical scenes, to create a place where traditions and modernity freely mingle.
* "RadioPi":doc-dev/radiopi.html, the radio of the École Centrale Paris
* "Radio Popolare Roma":http://www.radiopopolareroma.it/ is a free and independent FM radio in Rome, focused on news and culture. It is part of the Popolare Network, an Italian network of radios that share the same principles.
* "Radionomy":http://www.radionomy.com/ is a web platform hosting Internet radios, using Liquidsoap to stream more than 5000 radios :)
* "Radiotango Rosario":http://www.radiotangorosario.com.ar/ streams 24h a day of more than 10.000 tangos, microprograms, and history, in Spanish language, from Rosario in Argentina.
* "Radiozerozero":http://www.radiozerozero.com/ is a french radio playing DJ mixes.
* "Радио ВКонтакте":http://radiovkontakte.ru/ is a Russian Internet radio station broadcasting a wide range of electronic music from the world's best DJs and producers.
* "Riff":http://www.riff-radio.org/ a french webradio mainly focused on rock (especially hard rock), with daily live talk shows.
* "Robotstream":http://www.robotstream.com/ is a radio automatisation software written by Numeri'zik.
* "Rural Catholic Radio":http://www.ruralcatholicradio.com is a "proud (and very satisfied) savonet user".
* "SecurityCast Radio":http://www.securitycast.net/ is a radio that relays various computer security podcasts.
* "Savana Radio":http://savanaradio.com is a web-only radio from Sri Lanka, broadcasting Sinhala (Sri Lankan) music.
* "Radio Sing Sing":http://www.sing-sing.org/ is an independent FM and Internet radio based in St Malo, France, as well as an association organizing live concerts.
* "Substream.at":http://substream.at/, a radio for music lovers by music lovers, where you are invited to participate!

_And many more excellent but unknown projects... Don't hesitate to "tell us":mailto:savonet-users@lists.sf.net if you run a Liquidsoap radio so that we can enhance this list!_
